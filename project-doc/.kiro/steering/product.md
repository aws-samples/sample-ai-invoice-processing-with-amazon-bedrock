# Product Overview

## InvoiceFlow AI

InvoiceFlow AI is an intelligent, end-to-end invoice processing platform that transforms manual accounts payable operations into fully automated workflows. The platform leverages Amazon Bedrock's multimodal AI capabilities to process invoice images into verified, payment-ready transactions in under 2 minutes.

### Core Problem
Enterprise procurement teams manually process thousands of invoices monthly, with each requiring manual review, verification, and approval. This results in:
- Processing delays of 5-15 days per invoice
- High error rates requiring manual correction
- Strained vendor relationships due to payment delays
- Increased operational costs and resource allocation

### Solution
The platform combines:
- **Multimodal AI Processing**: Amazon Bedrock Foundation Models provide 95%+ accuracy on complex invoices
- **Knowledge Base Integration**: Stores and understands vendor contracts, pricing details, and business rules
- **AI Agents Orchestration**: Specialized agents handle vendor verification, compliance automation, and approval workflows
- **Real-time Reconciliation**: Automated purchase order matching and contract term validation

### Key Benefits
- Speed: Process invoices in under 2 minutes vs. 5-15 days
- Accuracy: 95%+ data extraction accuracy with contextual understanding
- Cost Reduction: 89% reduction in processing costs
- ROI: 634% in Year 1 with 1.8-month payback period
